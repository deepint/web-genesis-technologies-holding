About Us

Genesis Technologies Holding Limited was founded in 2021 with a global mission to identify and invest in emerging opportunities across various high-growth sectors. We specialize in cutting-edge fields such as artificial intelligence, green energy, and financial technology, where we see immense potential for innovation and impact.

As a venture capital fund, our approach is to not only provide financial backing but also to offer strategic support, leveraging our deep industry knowledge, resources, and technology to empower the growth of our portfolio companies. By fostering innovation and guiding startups through the challenges of scaling, we help them unlock their full potential.

Our team is driven by a shared vision to support transformative technologies that can address global challenges and shape the future. At Genesis Technologies Holding Limited, we are committed to creating long-term value for both our partners and the industries we invest in.
